Our Intention

Writing “Sri Rama” has been a sacred spiritual practice followed for generations.
With this Digital Ramakoti, wherever you are and whenever you wish,
you can easily participate in this divine seva.

After every 108 writings, a temple bell will ring and flowers will shower.
Your progress is safely stored and continues lifelong with accurate counts.

Especially designed for elderly devotees and those with vision difficulties:

• No typing required — just tap the large button to write “Jai Shri Ram”
• TalkBack support, large text, and high-contrast colors
• Vibration and temple bell sound after every chant
• Optional “Audio Mode” — Volume Up = Chant, Volume Down = Undo
• Works offline and automatically syncs later

Our resolve: to help everyone develop the habit of writing the sacred name of Sri Rama and deepen devotion.

Jai Shri Ram ✨
